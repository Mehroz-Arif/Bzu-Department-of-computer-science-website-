About the Department

Established: 1995. The Department provides an excellent educational environment that aims at bringing out the best. Our curriculum encompasses the recommendation of IEEE and ACM joint committee on CS Curriculum, National Curriculum Revision Committee, and duly approved by the HEC, MoE and MoST.

Why study with Us

Our students get Unlimited access to high-end computing facilities. The institute department houses Over 10 teaching laboratories / interactive multimedia classrooms plus speedy Wi-Fi availability for students' use.

messege form the chair

I would like to extend my warmest welcome to you as the Chair to the Department of Computer Science at Bhaduin zakria university.

Computing has revolutionized our world as we know it in the past two decades and will continue to serve as the core of all technologies in the 21st century. To prepare the future leaders for these innovations, I am proud to be the chair of a department which offers ABET accredited B.S. degree programs in Computer Science and Computer Engineering, an ABET accredited B.A. program in Information Technology, M.S. programs in Computer Science and Computer Engineering, and a Ph.D. program in Computer Science and Engineering, with a phenomenal faculty at the forefront of research in computing covering a wide range of areas to be selected for your interests. Our newest programs in Cybersecurity (B.S. and M.S.), as well as our M.S. degrees in Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering represent our continued efforts to prepare students for their careers, as well as to innovate in terms of research

There are so many ways you can be part of and achieve in the department: come visit us and find out what opportunities we can present you as a prospective student am always ready to have an exchange on any of the issues related to the building of the department and will try to make these topics equally exciting to you. I hope to see you here.

Minhaj Ahmad
